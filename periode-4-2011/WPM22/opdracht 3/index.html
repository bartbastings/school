Jonh Greyman's photography career began in the 1980’s in New York City galleries. After attending the North Caro- lina School of Arts, he moved to New York where he en- rolled at the Art Students League and the School of Visual Arts. His work caught the eye of his hero Andy Warhol, who offered him his first professional photography job.
