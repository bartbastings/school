Lacoste

Lacoste asked to help paint a vision of its catalog on mobile devices, through a ground-up redesign of its online experience. It resulted in a double app integrating catalog + lookbook. The navigation was simplified to its core, browsing through the products tree by pinching in and out. Tools like size selector appear on the fly. The motto was, re-introducing the sense of touch to shopping.
